## Concept

A performing arts center designed around a **parametric façade system** — a skin whose geometry is generated from design rules rather than drawn manually, letting the building envelope respond to performance criteria (daylighting, shading, structural rhythm, or acoustic zoning depending on the specific parameters used) instead of being a fixed decorative pattern.
